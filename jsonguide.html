JSON input guide

keytype(length)description
"name" String The player's last name.
"ss" Array(3) Serve Stats. Three numbers, indicating (1) First serve percentage; (2) First serve points won percentage; (3) Second serve points won percentage.
"sbd" Array(3) Serve Box Deuce. Three numbers, indicating percentage of serves aimed wide, central, or down-the-T. Absolute numbers can also be used.
"sbdwl" Array(6) Serve Box Deuce Win-Loss. Six numbers: the absolute number of points for wide serve win/loss, body serve win/loss, T serve win/loss.
"sbda" Array(3) Serve Box Deuce Aces. Three numbers: aces hit on wide, body, T serves on the deuce court.
"sba" Array(3) Serve Box Ad. Same format as sbd. The order here continues from left to right, i.e. T-serves, body serves, wide serves.
"sbawl" Array(6) Serve Box Ad Win-Loss. Same format as sbdwl. Ordered from T to wide, win-loss-win-loss-win-loss.
"sbaa" Array(3) Serve Box Ad Aces. Same format as sbda. Aces hit on T, body, wide serves on the Ad court.
"rs" Array(2) Return Stats. Two numbers: first is percent of returns in play, second is percent of return points won.
"rz" Array(9) Return Zones. Nine numbers indicating zones on the court where returns are hit to. The first three are shallow returns (inside service box), left middle and right. Then mid-depth returns, left middle and right. Then deep returns (last quarter of the court), left middle and right. Absolute numbers or percentages can be used.
"gz" Array(3) Groundstroke Zones. Three numbers, indicating percentage of groundstrokes aimed to the right/forehand corner (assuming righties), middle 40%, and left/backhand corner. Absolute numbers of shots also work.
"np" Array(2) Net Points. Two numbers, net points won, and total net points played.
